Our Services

We provide a comprehensive suite of eye care services, utilizing the latest technology to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Crystal Clear Vision

Advanced Cataract Surgery

Our state-of-the-art phacoemulsification (micro-incision) surgery ensures a painless experience with rapid visual recovery. We offer a range of premium intraocular lenses (IOLs) to suit your lifestyle needs.

  • Monofocal, Multifocal, and Toric IOLs
  • Bladeless Femtosecond Laser-Assisted Surgery
  • Quick, stitch-less procedure

Freedom from Glasses

LASIK & Refractive Surgery

Say goodbye to glasses and contact lenses. Our advanced laser vision correction procedures, including Contoura Vision and SMILE, are tailored to provide you with sharp, clear vision.

  • Comprehensive pre-operative evaluation
  • Customized treatment for each eye
  • Experienced refractive surgeons

Preserving Your Sight

Glaucoma Management

As a silent thief of sight, early detection of glaucoma is key. We offer complete diagnostic services and treatment options, from medication to advanced laser therapies and surgery, to protect your vision.

  • Tonometry & Perimetry (Visual Field Testing)
  • Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)
  • Laser Trabeculoplasty and Surgical options
Schedule a Screening
For Our Little Ones

Pediatric Ophthalmology

Children's eye care requires a special touch. Our dedicated pediatric specialists are experts in diagnosing and treating conditions like squint (strabismus), lazy eye (amblyopia), and refractive errors in children.

  • Child-friendly examination rooms
  • Comprehensive vision screening
  • Expert management of childhood eye diseases
Book for Your Child
Protecting Your Retina

Retina and Vitreous Services

We provide comprehensive care for retinal conditions such as diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, retinal detachments, and uveitis, using advanced diagnostic tools and treatment modalities.

  • Fundus Fluorescein Angiography (FFA)
  • Intravitreal Injections
  • Retinal Laser Photocoagulation & Vitrectomy Surgery
